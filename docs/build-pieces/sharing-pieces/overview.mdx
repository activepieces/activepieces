## Methods
- [Contribute Back](/build-piecessharing-pieces/contribute): Publish your piece by contributing back your piece to main repository.
- [Community](/build-piecessharing-pieces/community): Publish your piece on npm directly and share it with the community.
- [Private](/build-piecessharing-pieces/private): Publish your piece on activepieces privately.
