# Readwise

Readwise is your reading companion that syncs all your highlights from Kindle, web articles, Twitter, and more into one place.

## Authentication

Generate a personal access token at [readwise.io/access_token](https://readwise.io/access_token).

## Actions

- **Get Highlights** — Retrieve highlights from your library, filtered by book or date.
- **Create Highlight** — Save a new highlight to Readwise.

## Triggers

- **New Highlight** — Fires when a new highlight is added to your Readwise library.
